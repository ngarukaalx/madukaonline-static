Privacy Policy for MadukaOnline

Effective date: July 22, 2025

MadukaOnline ("we", "our", or "us") is a shop directory and marketplace platform that helps users discover and connect with shops around them and across the country.

Information We Collect

We collect the following personal and shop-related information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number (only for shop owners)
  • Location Data (latitude and longitude for shop discovery and listing)
  • Uploaded Media (images and videos uploaded publicly by shop owners)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Enable users to discover shops nearby or across the country
  • Allow shop owners to list and manage their shops
  • Improve app functionality, performance, and user experience

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect user data:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Database, Notifications)
  • Google Analytics (Usage tracking and app performance)
  • Expo / React Native tracking (App monitoring and error reporting)

Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Data Access and Security

We do not access or store private user chats. All publicly uploaded shop content (images/videos) is visible to other users.

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.

Children’s Privacy

Our app does not target or knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Your Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Delete their shop accounts and personal accounts at any time
  • Request removal of their personal data, where applicable

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes via the app or email.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at: support@madukaonline.co.ke
