Delete Your MadukaOnline Account

If you would like to delete your MadukaOnline account and remove all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Steps to Delete Your Account from the App

  1. Open the MadukaOnline app on your device.
  2. Go to your profile or settings screen.
  3. Tap on “Delete Account”.
  4. Confirm your request when prompted.

What Happens After Deletion?

When your account is deleted:

  • Your user profile is permanently removed.
  • Your shop listings (if any) are deleted.
  • Your email, phone number, and any associated metadata are erased.
  • This action is irreversible and your account cannot be restored.

Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to access your account through the app, you can send a request to delete your account manually by emailing:

support@madukaonline.co.ke

Data Retention

Once your request is processed, we delete your data within 7 days. Some data may be retained temporarily for legal or operational reasons, but will be purged automatically within 90 days.

For more information, please read our Privacy Policy.
